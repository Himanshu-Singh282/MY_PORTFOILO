Sales Dashboard

This Excel dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of income, expenses, and savings for the current month. It is designed to help you track financial activity, identify spending patterns, and make informed budgeting decisions. Key Features:
Income Tracking: Categorized view of all income sources.
Expense Breakdown: Visual charts showing spending by category (e.g., rent, groceries, utilities, etc.).
Budget vs. Actual: Comparison of planned budget vs. actual spending to highlight variances.
Savings Summary: Calculates remaining balance and savings achieved.
Interactive Filters: Monthly dropdowns and slicers for easy data navigation.
Visual Insights: Dynamic graphs and conditional formatting to quickly spot trends and overages.
